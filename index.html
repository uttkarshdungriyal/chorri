About Us @ BluO Amani – Boutique Hotel Goa

Our USP @ BluO Vagator Hotel in North Goa – Boutique accommodation to fit the needs of all guests (Rooms & Residences) ensuring privacy and top amenities compared to other Business Hotels & Guest Houses in Goa.

What are the typical guests staying at BluO Vagator Hotel Goa?
Designed for the discerning travellers looking for a different, more intimate accommodation, our boutique property consists of 28 spacious Rooms with Balcony & Suites having their own Living Room with besides Bedroom with attached Bathroom. The property has a Swimming Pool for all guests plus plenty of car parking besides offering mesmerizing views of Vagator beach & hills.

We also welcome Pets as long as guests ensure to keep them leashed and don’t discomfort other guests.

We are a non-discriminating hospitality company with transparent pricing and no hidden costs, so you can be assured of full value-for-money and world class services.

Latest News – Our In-House Concierge Team offers MICE facilities with Meetings & Conference Room packages that include meals. We are also taking bookings for Corporate Parties & Get-Togethers across different venues – Poolside & Party Hall.

* Our Corporate Guest House Management Team also specialises in setting up and managing dedicated Guest houses in Goa for Pan-India Companies having regular corporate travel needs at Goa.

 For arranging show-rounds of our property and to create your customised corporate stay solution, please do connect with our Sales Team today!
